Project Plan of Rocky Mountain Rail

OBJECTIVE

The Rocky Mountain Rail Authority (RMRA) High-Speed Rail Feasibility Study aims to assess the feasibility of implementing high-speed rail infrastructure in key corridors along I-70, I-25, and secondary routes. This Project Management Plan (PMP) provides a comprehensive framework for managing and executing the project.

INSIGHTS

The RMRA High-Speed Rail Feasibility Study demonstrates adaptability, highlighting its potential for significant economic benefits and regional prosperity. Despite challenges, the project's success underscores the viability of high-speed rail in the Rocky Mountain region, promising a more sustainable and connected future.
